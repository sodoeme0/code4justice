    Get Involved
    Looking to fill some spare time?

    How about filling it with some rewarding selfless time with us? We are looking for people like yourself with expertise in the following areas below!

    Construction Volunteer

    (individual or team) Work hands-on to change a life by making critical home repairs and accessibility upgrades such as ramps

    Homeowner Outreach

    You can make a difference just by letting a homeowner know a resource exists to help them. Promote WARM services to homeowners in need by placing mailbox hangers.

    Mission Trip

    WARM Ambassador

    Develop your leadership and project management skills by serving as the point of contact for the team from your business, church. club, or network of friends. Inspire, recruit, coordinate, and schedule your team to complete repairs.

    Photographer or Snack Hero

    Do you want to make a difference but don't have a lot of time to commit? Visit a WARM rebuild to take photos for use on social media and promotional material or deliver snacks to show appreciation to volunteer teams and homeowners.

    Office Volunteer

    Volunteers can assist with day to day clerical duties, such as answering phones and filing paperwork

    Committees of the Board

    Expand WARM's network in the community and get to know our board of directors. The purpose of our cultivation committees in to build WARM's relationships in specific sectors that will increase awareness and support WARM's mission. Committees include: Church & Civic, Corporate, Individuals, Government Affairs.
